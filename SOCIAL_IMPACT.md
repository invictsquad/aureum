# Aureum Social Impact — Democratizing AI

**Mission: Make AI accessible to everyone, everywhere, regardless of economic status**

---

## 🌍 The Problem

### Current State of AI
- **Expensive Hardware Required**
  - High-end GPUs: $500-$5,000
  - Powerful CPUs: $200-$1,000
  - Minimum 8GB RAM
  - Total cost: $1,000+ for AI-capable device

- **Geographic Inequality**
  - AI education concentrated in wealthy countries
  - Developing nations lack access to hardware
  - Cloud APIs require internet + payment
  - Creates "AI divide" between rich and poor

- **Environmental Impact**
  - Large models consume massive energy
  - Data centers contribute to carbon emissions
  - Unsustainable for global scale

### Who Gets Left Behind?
- 🌍 **3 billion people** with only basic smartphones
- 📚 **Students** in developing countries
- 🏥 **Healthcare workers** in remote areas
- 🌾 **Farmers** needing crop disease detection
- 🏭 **Small businesses** without IT budgets

---

## ✨ The Aureum Solution

### Technical Innovation
- **16x Less Memory** — 250MB vs 4GB for 1B parameter model
- **Zero FP Multiplications** — Runs on weakest CPUs
- **2-bit Weights** — BitNet b1.58 ternary quantization
- **Adaptive Scaling** — Matryoshka for any hardware

### Result: AI on $50 Phones
- ✅ Runs on ARM32 with 512MB RAM
- ✅ Works offline (no internet needed)
- ✅ Fast inference (<100ms)
- ✅ Low battery usage (integer-only math)
